Gabriel Chen's study focuses on Computer Security with an emphasis on Network Architecture and Infrastructure. They hold GFACT and GSEC for GIAC. They work as a Security Engineer who designs and reviews designs in low-level infrastructures.

Research Interests: Defensible System Security Architecture Design, Network and Information Transmission Security.

During year 2022 - 2023, Gabriel serves as the Vice Chair of ISOC Cybersecurity SIG.

Gabriel finished their undergraduate Computer Science journey at UC Berkeley, and they spent some time working with Chris Umans at Caltech on Catalytic Turing Machines afterwards.
